WebRTC Pairing — Quick Links

Use these links to open the Sender (remote phone) and Viewer (your laptop) pages. Open the sender on the remote device and the viewer on the machine where you want to watch.

Open sender.html (remote) Open viewer.html (viewer)

Quick instructions

  1. Serve these files over https or open via http://localhost to allow camera access.
  2. Open sender.html on the remote device (phone) and click Start Camera → Create Offer.
  3. Open viewer.html on your laptop, paste the offer, click Create Answer, then paste the answer back into sender and accept.
